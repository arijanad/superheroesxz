About us

Superheroes-XZ is the government's official journalistic website for children and teens between the ages of 10 and 15 years old. Superheroes-XZ was founded in 2099 and was initially only used as a source of information for children. It has since developed into so much more.

On this website, our talented team keeps you updated on the latest happenings on Earth-XZ. Our top journalists are Roos Deurloo, Panayiotis Michael, Ben Vickery and Arijana Dedusic. We also provide tips and tricks for figuring out your superpower and how to use it, as we know that our target audience is currently in that phase of their lives. All of this cool knowledge is accompanied by games, videos and puzzles in order to keep you entertained!

We have recently expanded our production team through a collaboration with famous podcaster Lilly Little, and her podcast Lilly Little - The podcast for heroes. Lilly Little's weekly podcast features a new superhero every week and discusses their personal lives and powers. Lilly Little is a bubbly and fun presenter whose quirky personality will draw you towards her. We will feature a brand new episode on our 'News' page every week, so look forward to that!

Something that we are very passionate about is our campaign, #EmpowerEveryone. You can read more about that here.
