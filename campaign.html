About #EmpowerEveryone

#EmpowerEveryone is an non-profit organization which was developed in 2110 by Superheroes-XZ. Our aim is to make people, primarily children, happy with the superpowers they have, whether it be a B grade power or a X grade power. This is done through workshops, presentations and live events. #EmpowerEveryone has been funded by various private organizations, but our main source of income comes from donations and merchandise.

Our newest addition to the campaign is ambassador Daniel Davids, a 13-year-old boy who is the first human in 100 years who does not possess the XZ gene which gives you superpowers. Daniel is hoping to spread his campaign message "It's okay to be different". Daniel's goal is to make sure that everyone knows that they are unique in their own way. Daniel plans on expanding his campaign through billboards and public speakers. We will keep you up to date on how his campaign further develops on our 'News' page.

Use the hashtags #EmpowerEveryone and #ItsOkayToBeDifferent on various social media platforms to let us know you support us and Daniel! Spread the message and create awareness!
