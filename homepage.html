BREAKING: Superpower-less Daniel Davids joins #EmpowerEveryone

Daniel Davids, the 13-year-old boy from North Dakota with a viral Instagram account with 74 million followers, has made an official statement on his Instagram page where he confirms the rumors of him not having a superpower to be true. Daniel Davids was tested by the IAA (International Ability Agency), who confirmed that he does not possess the XZ gene, which is what gives people superpowers. This is a historical event which has taken America by storm. We at Superheroes-XZ spoke to Daniel before he posted his statement, and we are proud to announce that we have officially made him ambassador of our pre-existing movement: #EmpowerEveryone. #EmpowerEveryone aims to make people happy with the abilities they already possess, but it has now been expanded with the message "It's okay to be different", which teaches children that everyone is special in their own way. More on #EmpowerEveryone can be read here. Please consider donating or buying our merchandise as your financial support will help us fund some incredible upcoming events.

BREAKING: DanielDavidsXZ, viral on Instagram for his funny videos

Daniel Davids, a 13-year-old boy from North Dakota has gone viral on Instagram.
